Why Sponsor?

Xenohuru is 100% free and open source. Sponsorships help cover server hosting, API costs, domain registration, and development time — keeping this platform alive and growing for Tanzania's tourism community.

Keep It Free

Your sponsorship ensures Xenohuru remains free for travellers, students, and tourism operators across Tanzania.

Fund Growth

Help us add more regions, attractions, Kiswahili translations, offline maps, and an improved mobile experience.

Get Recognized

Sponsors are featured on this page, in the project README, and can display their logo across the platform.
